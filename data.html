Welcome to
Big Data Astronomy

Get ready to explore one of the largest survey data sets ever created for Astronomy. We have powerful and easy to use tools to help you access, explore, and investigate the data collected by LSST, regardless of your experience and skill level.

Access Data with LSST Science Notebooks

With LSST's Science Notebook, you can search through real LSST data and use Python-based analysis tools through an easy-to-use online platform. You can share customized activities with your friends, collaborate to analyze data, and then publish and share your results to the world!

LSST Science Notebooks bring the future of astronomy and data science to you. Watch the video to learn more, check out our full tutorial series, or hop right in and create your first science notebook with our easy and fun step-by-step guide.
