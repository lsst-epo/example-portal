Intro to Web Notebooks

Web notebooks deliver real LSST data and interactive analysis tools to your students using a standard web interface. Assign customized investigations, collaborate to analyze data, and then publish and share your findings within your classroom — or with astronomers everywhere!

LSST Web Notebooks bring the future of astronomy and data science to your classroom. Watch the video to learn more, or check out our full tutorial series.
